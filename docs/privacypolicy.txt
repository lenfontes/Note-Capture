 
Privacy Policy for noteCapture

Effective Date: 1/1/2025

At Boomfont, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our noteCapture Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Authentication Information

We collect temporary OAuth 2.0 tokens to authenticate your Google account and securely access your Google Docs or Google Notebook. These tokens are not stored or shared and are used solely to facilitate the extension’s functionality.

Website Content

When you highlight text and choose to save it, the extension collects:
	•	The highlighted text.
	•	The URL of the webpage where the text was highlighted (optional, based on your settings).
This data is transmitted securely to your selected Google Doc or Notebook.

Web History (Limited)

The URL of the page where you interact with the extension is collected only to provide a reference link for the saved content.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
	•	Personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email address, or age).
	•	Health or financial information.
	•	Personal communications (e.g., emails or texts).
	•	Location data (e.g., GPS or IP address).
	•	General web history unrelated to extension use.
	•	User activity such as keystrokes, mouse movements, or clicks outside the extension.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:
	•	Authenticate your Google account for secure access to Google Docs or Notebook.
	•	Save highlighted text and associated metadata (e.g., URLs) to your chosen Google Doc or Notebook.

4. How We Protect Your Information

Your data is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption (e.g., HTTPS). We do not store any data on our servers or share your information with third parties. All interactions with Google services comply with their security and privacy standards.

5. Permissions and Data Usage

The extension requests the following permissions:
	•	activeTab: To interact with the content of the active tab where you highlight text.
	•	storage: To save user preferences like default sync source and categories.
	•	https://www.googleapis.com/: To access Google APIs for saving highlighted text to your Docs or Notebook.
	•	notifications: To inform you about the success or failure of saving highlights.

6. Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with Google services (Google Docs and Google Keep APIs). Your use of these services is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the extension or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be revised accordingly.
